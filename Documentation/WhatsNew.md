## 1. It's now an installable app (PWA) 📲

You no longer just open it in a browser tab — you can install it like a normal
app, and it keeps working without internet.

**Install it on your iPhone/iPad:**
1. Open the live link in **Safari**.
2. Go to the **Settings** tab → tap **📲 Install app** for step-by-step help, or
   just tap the **Share** button → **Add to Home Screen** → **Add**.
3. The **owl icon** appears on your home screen and opens full-screen, no address bar.

**Install it on Android / a computer:**
1. Open the live link in Chrome/Edge.
2. Go to **Settings** → tap **📲 Install app** → confirm the install dialog.

**Works offline:**
- After you've opened it once, it loads even with no signal — try turning on
  Airplane Mode and reopening it. Your data was always stored on your own device,
  so it's all still there.

## 3. Just for fun — hidden surprises 🎉

Little easter eggs, just because. (Not essential — feel free to skip, or go
hunting!)

- **Summon the owl:** on a computer, type the word **`athena`**; on a phone,
  **press and hold** the sun/moon mascot on the **Today** screen for ~1.5s. An
  owl glides across with a cheeky message.
- **New personal record:** finish a workout where you beat a past weight for an
  exercise → **confetti** + a "New personal record!" card.
- **Milestone trophy:** hit **7** completed workouts (and again at 30/50/100) →
  a one-time **🏆 celebration**.
- **Secret credits:** tap the app title (top-left) **5 times quickly** → a hidden
  card slides up.

---

## 4. Tidier behind the scenes 🧹

- **Deleting an exercise** now also cleans up its old workout history, so nothing
  stale is left behind. (The confirmation message tells you this — and a backup
  you exported earlier can always restore it.)
- **Deleting a workout** keeps your milestone trophies honest: if you drop below
  7 workouts and climb back up, the trophy can celebrate again.
