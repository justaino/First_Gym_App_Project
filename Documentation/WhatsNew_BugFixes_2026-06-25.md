# What's New 💪 — Insights & fixes (25 June 2026)

This round adds a brand-new **Insights** section to the Progress tab, lets you
**edit a workout's date**, and fixes how sets get recorded. Thanks for testing! 🙏

> Your data still lives only in your own browser on your own device (no accounts
> yet). Use **Settings → Export** to back it up or move it to another device.
> Insights are calculated from your own saved workouts, so the more you log, the
> more they show.

---

## 1. New: Insights on the Progress tab 📈

Open the **Progress** tab — there's a new **Insights** card at the top. Once you
have some finished workouts, it shows:

- **This week's goal ring** — a circle that fills toward your weekly workout goal.
  Set the goal in **Settings → Weekly goal** (default 3 per week).
- **Quick stats** — your current **week streak 🔥**, **days this month**, and
  lifetime totals (workouts, sets, reps, and total weight moved).
- **Personal records 🏅** — the heaviest weight you've hit for each exercise.
- **Last 12 weeks heatmap** — a little grid of squares, one per day, that get
  darker the more sets you did. **Tap a square** to see that day's details in a
  speech bubble (great on phones).
- **Volume this month** — your total work (reps × weight) this month, with a
  **↑ / ↓ % vs last month**.
- **Since you started** — how much each exercise has gone **up or down** in weight
  since your first session with it.

**Try it:** do (or edit) a few workouts with weights over different days, then open
**Progress** and explore the Insights card. Tap the heatmap squares too.

---

## 2. New: Edit a workout's date 📅

When you start or **Edit** a workout, there's now a **Date** field at the top.
Changing it moves the workout to that day — and the wording updates to match (move
it to a Friday and it becomes a **"Friday workout"** in your history).

**Try it:** On the **Today** tab, find a finished workout under **Recent workouts**
→ **Edit** → change the **Date** → **Finish**. Check that it shows the new date and
day name in your history.

---

## 3. Fix: only the sets you tick "done" are recorded ✅

Previously, if you started a workout and **finished without ticking any sets**, it
could still be saved — and could even wrongly celebrate a "new personal record"
using weights you never actually did.

Now:
- A workout **won't finish** unless you've ticked **at least one set** as done
  (you'll get a friendly nudge, or you can **Discard**).
- **Only ticked sets count** toward your sets, reps, weights, records, and Insights.

**Try it:** Start a workout, tick **nothing**, and tap **Finish** → it should ask
you to tick a set first (no phantom record). Then tick one set and finish normally.
