# User Guide 💪 — Getting Started

Welcome! This is a simple, friendly gym app that lives right in your web browser.
You set up your exercises for each day of the week, then run through them as a
workout — ticking off sets, logging your weights, and watching your progress grow.

No account, no install, nothing to download. Let's go. 👇

---

## Quick start (60 seconds)
1. **Open the app** using the link you were sent.
2. Go to the **Settings** tab → type your name → tap **Create** (that's your profile).
3. Go to the **Schedule** tab → tap **＋ Add exercise** → fill it in → **Save**.

That's it — you're set up. The rest of this guide explains everything in detail.

---

## A few things to know first
- It works on your **phone or computer** browser — just open the link.
- Your data is saved **only in your own browser, on your own device.** Nothing is
  uploaded anywhere, and no one else can see it. (If you clear your browser data,
  the app starts fresh — see **Back up your data** below to keep a copy.)
- At the bottom you'll always see four tabs: **Today · Schedule · Progress · Settings.**

---

## 1. Create your profile
1. Tap the **Settings** tab (the ⚙️ at the bottom).
2. Under **New profile name**, type your name and tap **Create**.
3. Your profile is now active — you'll see it in the top-right corner.

You can have more than one profile (e.g. for a friend or a different routine) and
switch between them anytime. Each profile keeps its own exercises and history.

---

## 2. Add your exercises
Think of this as building your weekly plan. For each exercise:

1. Go to the **Schedule** tab and tap **＋ Add exercise**.
2. Fill in:
   - **Name** — e.g. "Push-ups", "Squats".
   - **Sets** — how many sets (e.g. 3).
   - **Default reps** — reps per set (e.g. 10). This fills every set for you.
   - **Default weight (optional)** — leave blank for bodyweight exercises, or enter
     a number (e.g. 20). This fills every set's weight.
   - **Reps & weight per set** — each set has its own row. They start from your
     defaults, but you can change any individual set (e.g. 10, 12, 15 reps).
   - **Icon** — pick a fun emoji.
   - **Day** — which day of the week it belongs to.
3. Tap **Save.**

Add as many as you like. Tip: when you add several in a row, it keeps the same day,
so you can quickly build out, say, all of Monday.

**Edit or remove an exercise:** on the Schedule tab, tap **Edit** or **Delete** on
any exercise card.

---

## 3. See your plan
- **Schedule tab** — all your exercises grouped by day of the week.
- **Today tab** — just what's planned for today, with a friendly greeting and a
  **▶ Start workout** button when you've got something to do.

---

## 4. Do a workout
This is where you actually train. Your progress **saves automatically** as you go,
so you can stop and come back without losing anything.

1. On the **Today** tab, tap **▶ Start workout** (or use **▶ Start** next to any day
   on the Schedule tab).
2. Each exercise shows a row for every set, with the **reps** and **weight** already
   filled in from your plan.
3. As you train:
   - Tap the **circle** on a set to mark it **done** (it turns green ✓).
   - Change the **reps** or **weight** for any set if today was different — this only
     affects today's workout, not your saved plan.
   - Tap **＋ Add set** to add an extra set, or **✕** to remove one (today only).
4. **Rest timer:** tap **60s / 90s / 120s** for a countdown between sets — it beeps
   when time's up (make sure your phone isn't on silent 🔔).
5. When you're done, tap **Finish.** Your workout is saved to your history.

**Need to stop partway?** Tap **Close** — your progress is kept. The button will
then say **▶ Resume workout**, so you can pick up right where you left off (even
after closing the browser). To throw a workout away, tap **Discard.**

> Your saved exercises (the plan) never change from doing a workout — so "Push-ups
> 3 × 10" stays the same for next time, even if today you did 4 sets.

---

## 5. Track your progress
Tap the **Progress** tab to see how you're doing:
- **This week** — how many workouts and sets you've done.
- **By exercise** — a small chart for each exercise. Each workout shows two bars:
  a **mint** bar for **reps** and a **lavender** bar for **weight**. Watch the
  lavender bars climb as you lift heavier! 💪
- **Tap any bar** to see the full details of that workout.

---

## 6. Edit or delete a past workout
Made a mistake, or want to tidy up your history?
- On the **Today** tab, find the workout under **Recent workouts.**
- Tap **Edit** to reopen and change it (then **Finish**), or **Delete** to remove it.

---

## 7. Switch profiles
On the **Settings** tab, tap **Use** next to any profile to make it active, or
**Create** to add another. The top-right corner always shows who's active.

---

## 8. Light or dark mode
Tap the **🌙 / ☀️** button in the top-right to switch between light and dark. The
little mascot turns into a sun or a moon to match. Your choice is remembered.

---

## 9. Back up your data (important!)
Because your data lives only in your browser, it's worth keeping a copy — especially
before clearing your browser, or to move it to another device.

On the **Settings** tab, under **Backup:**
- **Export this profile** or **Export all profiles** — saves a file to your device.
- **Import** — loads a backup file back in. Importing **merges** profiles: a new one
  is added, and one you already have is updated, leaving your others untouched.

---

## Quick FAQ
- **Do I need internet?** Only to open the app the first time. After that it works
  offline in your browser.
- **Will my friend see my data?** No — everyone's data stays on their own device.
- **I cleared my browser and lost everything!** That's why the **Export** backup
  exists 🙂 — keep a file somewhere safe.
- **Something looks wrong.** Note what you did and what happened, and let the person
  who shared the app know — it really helps.

Enjoy your training! 🏋️
