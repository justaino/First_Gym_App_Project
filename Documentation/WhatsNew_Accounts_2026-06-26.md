# What's New 💪 — Accounts & cloud sync (26 June 2026)

The big one! The Justaino Gym App now has **accounts**, so your workouts **sync across
your devices** and survive clearing your browser or switching phones. Thanks for
testing! 🙏

> ⚠️ **The main change:** you now **log in** to use the app. The very first time you
> log in, any workouts you already had on this device are **saved to your account
> automatically** — you won't lose anything.

---

## 1. New: sign up / log in 🔑

When you open the app you'll see a **login screen**.

- **First time?** Tap **Sign up**, enter your **email** and a **password** (at least
  6 characters). You're logged straight in — no confirmation email to wait for.
- **Coming back?** Enter the same email + password and tap **Log in**.
- **Log out** anytime in **Settings → Log out** (top of the page).

**Try it:** sign up with your email, then close the app and reopen it — you should
still be logged in.

---

## 2. New: your workouts sync across devices ☁️

Your data now lives in the **cloud** (not just this one browser), so:

- Log in on your **phone** and your **laptop** and you'll see the **same**
  profiles, exercises, and history on both.
- Clear your browser, switch phones, or use a brand-new device — log in and
  **everything's still there**.

**Try it:** add an exercise on one device, then log in with the same account on
another device (or another browser) — it should show up. Changes you make sync
back the same way.

**First-login note:** the first time you log in on a device that already had
workouts, you'll see a one-time message confirming your existing data has been
saved to your account.

---

## 3. Works offline (mostly) 📡

The app still works without a connection:

- **Viewing** your plan and history works offline.
- **Doing a workout** works offline — it saves and syncs up when you're back online.
- **Editing your plan** (adding/editing/deleting profiles or exercises) **needs a
  connection** — if you're offline you'll get a friendly "you're offline" message
  instead of a confusing error. Just reconnect and try again.

---

## 4. New: privacy & data controls 🔒

In **Settings → Privacy & data** you'll find:

- A plain-English note on **what's stored** (your email + your workout data) and
  **where** (Supabase, a trusted third-party cloud service). Only you can see your
  data. There's a **"Read the full privacy note"** link too.
- A **"Delete my data"** button that permanently removes **all** your profiles,
  exercises, and history from the cloud and your device. (Tip: export a backup
  first if you want a copy — **Settings → Backup**.)

---

## A few things to know

- **Forgot your password?** There's **no self-serve password reset yet** (it's
  coming in a later update). For now, keep your password somewhere safe — and if
  you do get locked out, just message me.
- **Want your account fully removed?** "Delete my data" clears your workouts; to
  delete the **login itself**, message me and I'll remove it.
- **First load might be slow:** the free cloud database naps after about a week of
  no use and takes a few seconds to wake up. Give it a moment.
- **Backups still work** exactly as before (Settings → Backup) if you like keeping
  your own copy.
